[7|Heading 2] SUMMARY OF COVER
[8|Body Text] This is a guide to the cover given by the policy. It does not cover every provision of the policy wording, but your broker will be happy to discuss it with you in more detail. You should read the policy and the additional clauses for full details of the terms and conditions.  This Policy is a commercial insurance product designed for business customers and is not suitable for consumers.
